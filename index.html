What is CFW?

CFW started as a solo eFed role-playing game built with the help of ChatGPT. I handle the booking, storylines, and creative direction, while a dice system decides the winners — forcing me to book around the results. Every show is written in an old-school Word-doc style, complete with AI-generated visuals to bring the world at The Foundry to life. It’s a blend of storytelling, luck, and imagination — part RPG, part wrestling show.

CFW is open to contributors, role-players, and creative minds who want to help the world grow — whether through story ideas, artwork, or new character concepts. It’s meant to evolve with community input and imagination.
